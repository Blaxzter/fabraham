## TU Berlin — B.Sc.

A computer-science degree at TU Berlin (final grade 1.8) — but the formative work
happened alongside it. At **GT-ARC** I worked on multi-agent systems; as a student
developer at **RWTH Aachen** I helped build the identity-management apps used by
~**47,000 students and 9,000 staff**. One habit formed early: build software real
institutions actually run on.
