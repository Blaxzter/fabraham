## TU Berlin — B.Sc. Computer Science

The foundation: a computer-science degree at TU Berlin (final grade 1.8). The
interesting work happened alongside it. At **GT-ARC** I worked on multi-agent
systems; as a student developer at **RWTH Aachen** I helped build the
identity-management apps used by roughly **47,000 students and 9,000 staff**, and
an internship had me doing outlier detection on file-server backups.

Different problems, one habit forming early: make software that real institutions
actually run on.
