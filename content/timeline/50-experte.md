## Experte — retrieval-augmented generation

At Respeak I'm the **tech lead** on Experte (respeak.io), and I built the core of
it. Experte is a **RAG** — retrieval-augmented generation — platform for public
administration: it answers questions grounded in an organisation's own documents,
the PDFs, SharePoint, and Confluence already sitting inside the building.

The whole point is trust. Instead of a model guessing, Experte retrieves the
relevant passages and returns **cited answers** — every claim traceable back to
the source it came from. Picture documents streaming in on one side and a sourced
answer beaming back out on the other; that flow is the product.

This is where the through-line finally closes. GANs learning to *generate* at
Maastricht, sentence embeddings turning meaning into vectors at Tatort, and now
*retrieval over those embeddings* — finding the right passage and grounding a real
answer in it. Same idea, one more turn: let the model learn structure from data,
then put that structure to work.
