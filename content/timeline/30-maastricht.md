and went all-in on generative models. My **thesis trained GANs to generate
procedural game content** (procedural content generation via machine learning) —
teaching a network to invent playable levels.

This is where the through-line starts: a lattice of weights learning to
*generate*. The same idea — let a model learn structure from data — comes back at
every stop after this.
