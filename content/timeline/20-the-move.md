## The move — Berlin to Maastricht

In 2020 I left Berlin for Maastricht. The B.Sc. was behind me, and the
Netherlands held an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence — a deliberate bet to stop
skimming machine learning and go all the way under.

This is the first leg of the loop: Berlin out, Maastricht across the border,
Berlin again at the far end. A threshold, not a destination. The arc bends south,
and everything that follows is what I carried back.
